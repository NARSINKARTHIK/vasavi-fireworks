🛡️ Stay Safe

Safety Instructions

Your safety is our top priority. Please follow these guidelines when using fireworks.

👁️

Always supervise children. Never let children handle fireworks alone.

🔥

Keep a bucket of water or sand nearby at all times when lighting fireworks.

👐

Light fireworks at arm's length and move away immediately after lighting.

🌬️

Never use indoors. Always use fireworks in open, ventilated outdoor areas.

👗

Wear cotton clothing. Keep loose or synthetic clothing away from fireworks.

♻️

Dispose used fireworks in water before discarding. Never re-light dud fireworks.

🌳

Keep fireworks away from dry grass, trees and flammable materials.
